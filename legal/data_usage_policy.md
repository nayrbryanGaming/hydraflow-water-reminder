# Data Usage Policy for HydraFlow

**Last Updated:** April 19, 2026

HydraFlow implements a "Privacy-First" mandate. This document clarifies how we utilize data to improve your hydration journey.

## 1. Minimal Data Collection
We only collect telemetry essential for app performance and behavioral feedback:
*   **Hydration Logs:** Timestamp and volume of liquid intake.
*   **Goal Progress:** Percentage of daily goal achieved.
*   **Device Specs:** OS version and model (for crash debugging).

## 2. Zero Sale of Personal Data
**We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal health data to third parties.** We do not use your hydration history for targeted advertising.

## 3. Analytics Transparency
We use Firebase Analytics to track feature usage (e.g., "how many users use the smart quiz?"). All analytics are aggregated and anonymized.

## 4. Local Processing
Where possible, calculations for your hydration velocity and habit scores are performed on-device to minimize cloud exposure.

## 5. Right to be Forgotten
Users can initiate a complete and recursive data purge via the **Danger Zone** in settings, satisfying global GDPR and CCPA standards.
