# Data Usage Policy

HydraFlow believes in complete transparency regarding how your data is used and stored.

## Data Processing
All data processing regarding goal calculations, streak checking, and progress mapping happens natively on your device or securely via Firebase Cloud Functions.

## Analytics
We track anonymous events (e.g., "water_logged", "goal_completed") to improve platform gamification mapping. This data cannot be traced back to your true identity and contains no personally identifiable information (PII).

## Sale of Data
**We guarantee that we do not sell your personal data.** 
HydraFlow's business model relies on Premium subscriptions, not data brokerage.
