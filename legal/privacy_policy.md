# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: April 25, 2026**

HydraFlow ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information in our mobile application.

## 1. 100% Offline-First Architecture
HydraFlow is designed with a strict **Offline-First** mandate. This means:
- **No Cloud Storage**: All your hydration logs, biometrics (weight, age), and settings are stored strictly on your local device.
- **No Accounts**: You do not need to create an account to use HydraFlow.
- **No Tracking**: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking pixels (e.g., Firebase, Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics).

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect
Because HydraFlow operates entirely on your device, we do NOT collect, access, or store the following on any external servers:
- Biometric data (weight, age, gender)
- Hydration history and patterns
- Device identifiers or location data
- Personal identifiers (name, email)

## 3. Data Sovereignty
You have total control over your data:
- **Data Portability**: You can export your hydration history as a JSON file at any time from the Settings menu.
- **Data Erasure**: Deleting the HydraFlow application or using the "Reset Data" feature in Settings will permanently wipe all local data from your device.

## 4. Security
Your data is stored in a local NoSQL database (Hive) within your application's private storage directory. It is protected by your device's native operating system security.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Any changes will be posted within the application.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@hydraflow.app.
