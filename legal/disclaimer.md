# Medical Disclaimer for HydraFlow

**Last Updated:** April 19, 2026

## 1. Not Medical Advice
HydraFlow is designed to be a behavioral habit-building tool. The content, hydration goals, and reminders provided by HydraFlow are for informational purposes only and do NOT constitute professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

## 2. No Doctor-Patient Relationship
Your use of HydraFlow does not create a doctor-patient relationship. The "Smart Assessment Quiz" uses population health datasets (NAM/WHO) to provide general guidelines, which may not be appropriate for your specific health condition.

## 3. Consult Your Physician
Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read in HydraFlow.

## 4. Emergency Situations
**Do not use HydraFlow for medical emergencies.** If you are experiencing symptoms of severe dehydration, heatstroke, or any other medical emergency, call emergency services (e.g., 911) immediately.

## 5. Limitation of Accuracy
While we strive for clinical integrity based on published health standards, we make no guarantees regarding the accuracy of the automated hydration goal calculations.
