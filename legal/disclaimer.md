# Medical Disclaimer

**IMPORTANT: READ BEFORE USING HYDRAFLOW**

HydraFlow is a wellness and habit-building application. It is **not** a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical condition.

## 1. Not a Substitute for Medical Advice
The hydration targets calculated by HydraFlow are based on general population standards (National Academies of Medicine and WHO guidelines). These calculations do not account for specific medical conditions, medications, or individual health requirements.

## 2. Consult Your Physician
Before significantly changing your fluid intake, especially if you have any of the following, consult a medical professional:
- Kidney disease or renal impairment
- Congestive heart failure
- Electrolyte imbalances
- Any condition requiring fluid restriction

## 3. Listen to Your Body
Thirst is a critical physiological signal. Do not override your body's natural signals based solely on application reminders. 

## 4. Assumption of Risk
By using HydraFlow, you acknowledge and agree that you are using the application at your own risk and that you are responsible for your own health decisions.
